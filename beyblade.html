Brief History of Beyblade

Beyblade is a line of spinning battle top toys which is currently owned by Takara Tomy and Hasbro. There is also a Beyblade anime and manga series. The first Beyblade toy was released in 1999. There are four types of beyblades, which battle in what is an advanced rock paper scissors style game. These four types are:

  • Attack: Outperforms stamina
  • Defense: Deflects and defeats attack
  • Stamina: Outlasts defense
  • Balance: Jack of all trades
